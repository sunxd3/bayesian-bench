# H5N1 Viral Kinetics in Dairy Cattle

Piecewise-linear viral kinetics model with data augmentation censoring across 4 heterogeneous data sources.

## Citation

Eales O, McCaw JM, Shearer FM (2026). Modeling of H5N1 influenza virus kinetics during dairy cattle infection suggests the timing of infectiousness. *PLOS Biology* 24(1): e3003586.

## Links

## Metadata

- **PPL**: Stan (RStan)
- **Language**: R
- **Published**: Jan 2026
- **Scale**: 19 cows from 4 studies, 6 model variants, 4 different censoring thresholds
