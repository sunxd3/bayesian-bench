# H5N1 Viral Kinetics in Dairy Cattle

Piecewise-linear viral kinetics with multi-study data integration, heterogeneous censoring thresholds, and errors-in-variables Ct-to-infectiousness mapping.

## Citation

Eales O, McCaw JM, Shearer FM (2026). Modeling of H5N1 influenza virus kinetics during dairy cattle infection suggests the timing of infectiousness. *PLOS Biology* 24(1):e3003586.

## Links

## Metadata

- **PPL**: Stan (RStan)
- **Language**: R
- **Published**: Jan 2026
- **Scale**: 19 cows from 4 studies, ~150-200 observations, 6 Stan models (1 primary + 4 sensitivity + 1 Ct-to-titer)
