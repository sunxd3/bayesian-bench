# Physiological Pharmacokinetic Analysis of Perchloroethylene

Population PBPK modeling of tetrachloroethylene (perchloroethylene) metabolism with informative prior distributions from physiological literature.

## Citation

Gelman A, Bois F, Jiang J (1996). Physiological pharmacokinetic analysis using population modeling and informative prior distributions. *Journal of the American Statistical Association* 91(436):1400-1412.

## Links

- **Paper**: https://doi.org/10.1080/01621459.1996.10476708
- **PDF**: https://sites.stat.columbia.edu/gelman/research/published/bois2.pdf
- **Reference implementation**: GNU MCSim `examples/perc/` — https://github.com/HSL/MCSim

## Original data source

Monster AC, Boersma G, Steenweg H (1979). Kinetics of tetrachloroethylene in volunteers; influence of exposure concentration and work load. *International Archives of Occupational and Environmental Health* 42(4):303-309.

## Metadata

- **PPL**: GNU MCSim (no Stan port exists)
- **Language**: MCSim model specification language
- **Published**: 1996
- **Scale**: 6 subjects, 2 exposure levels (72 and 144 ppm), exhaled air + venous blood time series
